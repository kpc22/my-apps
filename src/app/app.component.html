Kevin's Angular Projects

-> Disc Golf Scoring App <-

This app will make scoring simple and flexible. Users can keep score on their phone and record all of their throw distances with a few simple touches. A map building feature will be used to generate an image of the hole layout complete with tee and basket locations, hazards or out-of-bounds elements such as trees, water, islands,... and of course your discs - which will illustrate how you were able to birdie that long par 3.

-> GPS App <-

This app is used to measure gps coordinates and test the accuracy utilizing the browser's GPS data.
